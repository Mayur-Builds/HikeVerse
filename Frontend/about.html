About HikeVerse

HikeVerse is a full-stack trek discovery and safety planning web application designed to help users explore trekking destinations across Maharashtra.

The platform allows users to view trek details, check difficulty levels, see real-time weather conditions, save favorite treks, and share reviews and ratings based on their trekking experience.

HikeVerse is built to solve a real-world problem: many beginner trekkers struggle to find reliable trek information, weather updates, safety details, and planning resources in one place.

Key Features

  • ✅ User Signup and Login
  • ✅ Add, Edit, Delete, and View Treks
  • ✅ Real-Time Weather Information
  • ✅ Favorites System
  • ✅ Reviews and Ratings
  • ✅ Trek Safety and Planning Information
  • ✅ Dashboard Statistics
